Rock Paper Scissors

To play this game:

  1. Click the "Show Canvas" button to open camera and canvas
  2. Click "Start Game" and show the shape you form using one hand in front of your camera
  3. Click "Start Game" again to see the shape computer makes and the result
  4. Replay by repeating steps 2 and 3 multiple times
